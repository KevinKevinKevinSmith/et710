Olimar & Pikmin

Olimar is the poster character for the Pikmin series, a franchise that started on Gamecube but had its most recent addition release on Wii U.

Olimar is an astronaut that works for a space delivery company. In the first Pikmin game he crash lands onto an uncharted alien planet, where he discovers the Pikmin. The small creatures are at the lowest rung of the food chain and primarily eat nectar and seeds. However, with a crafty enough leader sporting a good throwing arm, Olimar quickly put the Pikmin at the top of the food chain all across the planet. All while finding the missing parts of his spaceship in order to go home, of course.

In Smash, Olimar needs Pikmin to use all but 6 of his attacks. Pikmin management is paramount. He is also one of the five lightest characters in the game, giving extra incentive to play a keep away game. However, he has a number of mixups from throwing various colored Pikmin, and if a few latch on he can quickly jump in for an aerial, plant a forward smash, or sneak in a grab to give the latched Pikmin extra time to deal bonus damage. Each Pikmin color also has different stats from attack/throw distance, basic attack power, grab power, and even latch damage.

As a light projectile character, Olimar has it rough. He has to play safer than most characters at times. But he carries one of the best recoveries in the game, is a projectile character with a great ranged grab and kill throw, is one of the best damage rackers in the game with simple attack and throw combos, and as a bonus you can play as an alternate character with the same stats: Alph from Pikmin 3. Olimar is a great character; you just have to remember not to jump headfirst into a brawl.
