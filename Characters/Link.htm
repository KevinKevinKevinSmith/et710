Link

Link is the star character from The Legend of Zelda series, a series known for producing its game covers with gold colors.

Link is known as the Hero of Time, a hero that appears whenever the forces of darkness, often led by long time series antagonist Ganondorf, gain too much of a foothold across the land of Hyrule. The series started with the ideas of carving your way through a hostile land and plumbing the depths of deadly dungeons, but has since become a lot more story driven and puzzle focused in recent releases.

In Smash, Link has various weapons that appear in a majority of his games. His bow and arrow is surprisingly powerful and great to use as a ranged poke, his bombs provide multi directional pressure, and his boomerang acts as a quick ranged jab that can anti-air or even pull enemies in. Along with all of that, Link also carries the Master Sword, which can cause a surprising amount of damage and knockback. His forward smash has a second hit, his up special whirlwind can be charged on the ground, and his dash attack can even kill early if you land it with the tip of the sword. Use projectiles to trip up approaching enemies while giving the real damage through your sword.
