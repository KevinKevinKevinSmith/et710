Rosalina & Luma

Rosalina is a character from the Mario Bros. franchise that has appeared in many games, but debuted in the critically acclaimed Super Mario Galaxy.

Rosalina is a goddess that watches over the creation and death of various heavenly bodies, from planets, to comets, to stars. The basic building block of all of these things are the Lumas, small star shaped creatures that Rosalina watches over and protects. In turn, the Lumas lend her their strength.

In Smash, Rosalina fights in tandem with various colored Lumas, although only one can be out with her at a time. The Lumas copy her moves and provide extra damage, knockback, and range to all of Rosalina’s attacks. The strength this provides her has left her as one of the lighter characters in the game, but that weakness is well deserved. Luma will often surprise opponents and interrupt grabs until enemies wizen up and attack them too. But even with the death of a Luma, another will be soon be born to replace the last.

The range that Luma provides allows Rosalina to be a zoning character, keeping opponents away from her light body. When Luma is away, she still keeps some range with her smash attacks and aerials, and has pretty good grab options to help waste some extra time. Early in Smash 4’s life, Rosalina was considered the best character in the game, so if you’re using her you should act like it.
