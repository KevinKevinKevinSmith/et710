Character Guides

My Favorite Smashers, grouped together

Zoning Characters

Prefer to keep characters just out of range with their superior reach. Outside of pokes/jabs/fast aerials they also like to mixup with grabs. They all have defensive moves to deal with various types of pressure.

Projectile Characters

Prefer to mixup with projectiles and close combat attacks, weaving back and forth between close and long range. Dislike grabbing and instead try to trip opponents up with levels of pressure. Often have few tools to deal with opponent projectiles.

Brawler Characters

Prefer to jump in with grabs, mixups, and shield pressure. Rushdown types. Can deal with projectiles without slowing down. Love to grab, and may even have a kill move or low percent combo off their grabs. Have a basic projectile to help counter characters at range or rushdown.
